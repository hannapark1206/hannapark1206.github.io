<div class="project-card">
<h3>Flight-Ready UAV Sensing and Control Integration</h3>
<p><strong>2024–2026 · Gharib Group / CAST / GALCIT</strong></p>
<p>Integrated embedded sensing, flight-control, actuator, and ground-station systems for fixed-wing aerial robot testing. Designed and wind-tunnel calibrated five-hole Pitot probe hardware for aerodynamic flow measurement.</p>
<p><strong>Tools:</strong> ROS, MAVLink/MAVROS, Cube Orange+, Jetson, Teensy, QGroundControl, OptiTrack, wind-tunnel calibration.</p>
</div>

<div class="project-card">
<h3>Low-Reynolds-Number Flow Facility</h3>
<p><strong>2024 · Gharib Group / Experimental Fluids</strong></p>
<p>Worked on compact water-tunnel hardware, modular turbine-array fixtures, electronics, power configuration, Arduino/PWM control, and PIV/dye-flow validation.</p>
<p><strong>Tools:</strong> Arduino, PWM control, turbine arrays, wiring, power configuration, PIV, dye visualization.</p>
</div>

<div class="project-card">
<h3>Real-Time Vision and Motion Control</h3>
<p><strong>2023 · Robotics / Computer Vision</strong></p>
<p>Built a camera-guided tracking system using Python/OpenCV target detection and HEBI actuator control for real-time pan–tilt motion.</p>
<p><strong>Tools:</strong> Python, OpenCV, HEBI actuators, target tracking, motion control.</p>
</div>

<div class="project-card">
<h3>Cryogenic Detector Hardware and Resonator Analysis</h3>
<p><strong>2024 · Golwala Group / SURF</strong></p>
<p>Worked on phonon-mediated kinetic inductance detector hardware, quasiparticle phonon guides, CAD mounting, wire-bonded assembly, and Python resonance fitting.</p>
<p><strong>Tools:</strong> CAD, wire bonding, KIDs, Sonnet/simulation, Python, least-squares fitting.</p>
</div>
