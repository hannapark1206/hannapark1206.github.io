## Overview

This project focused on phonon-mediated kinetic inductance detector hardware for sub-GeV dark matter detection.

My work combined detector design, CAD hardware, wire-bonded assembly, simulation, and Python-based resonator analysis.

## My Role

I worked on cryogenic detector hardware, KID geometry modifications, phonon collection structures, detector mounting, and resonance fitting.

## What I Worked On

- Modified kinetic inductance detector geometry
- Added quasiparticle phonon-guide concepts to improve phonon collection
- Redesigned detector mounting hardware
- Wire-bonded resonators for cryogenic operation
- Built a Python resonance-fitting pipeline for superconducting resonators
- Compared simulated and theoretical resonant-frequency behavior

## Technical Areas

KIDs, cryogenic detector hardware, CAD, wire bonding, Python, regression, least-squares fitting, resonator analysis.
