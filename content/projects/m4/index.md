## Senior thesis project

This project focuses on a multi-modal air–ground robotic system. My work connects CAD, fabrication, propulsion integration, avionics integration, thrust-biasing deflector design, and bench testing.

## My Role

I work on the mechanical and systems-integration side of the platform, with an emphasis on turning design concepts into testable hardware.

## What I Built and Integrated

<div class="two-col">

<div>

### Aerial hardware platform

- Built and integrated drone-platform hardware for air–ground testing
- Designed and fabricated structural components
- Worked through packaging, fit, and assembly constraints
- Iterated hardware based on test behavior and integration issues

</div>

<div>

### Deflector and testing work

- Designed thrust-biasing deflector concepts
- Used CFD-informed checks to compare design behavior
- Supported bench testing for thrust and drag tradeoffs
- Connected simulation, prototyping, and hardware validation

</div>
