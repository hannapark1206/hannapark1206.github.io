## Overview

This was my first research experience at Caltech through FSRI in the Rangel Neuroeconomics Laboratory.

The project studied visual search and choice behavior using behavioral and eye-tracking data. 

## What I Worked On

- Cleaned and organized behavioral data
- Used Python for analysis and plotting
- Applied regression analysis to study attention and choice difficulty
- Compared familiar and novel item behavior
- Presented the work in a research symposium and poster session

## Technical Areas

Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, regression analysis, data visualization, behavioral data, research presentation.
