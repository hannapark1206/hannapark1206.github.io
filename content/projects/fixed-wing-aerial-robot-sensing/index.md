# Fixed-Wing Aerial Robot Sensing and Flight-Control Integration

## Overview

This project focused on preparing a fixed-wing aerial robot for controlled flight experiments by integrating sensing, flight-control hardware, actuator systems, and ground-station communication.

The system combined aerodynamic sensing hardware with onboard computation and autopilot communication, allowing pressure, IMU, motion-capture, and flight-control data to be collected during testing.

## My Role

I worked on embedded sensing, flight-control integration, aerodynamic instrumentation, and experimental setup.

## What I Worked On

- Integrated flight-control, onboard-compute, actuator, and ground-station systems
- Built ROS/MAVLink workflows for data retrieval and communication
- Supported manual override and free-flight readiness
- Synchronized OptiTrack motion-capture data for real-time tracking
- Designed and calibrated five-hole Pitot probe hardware for aerodynamic flow measurement

## Technical Areas

ROS, MAVLink/MAVROS, Cube Orange+, Jetson, Teensy, QGroundControl, MAVProxy, OptiTrack, pressure sensing, wind-tunnel calibration, fixed-wing UAV testing.
